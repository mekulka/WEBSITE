Task

1. You will independently answer five questions from the video titled "The Rise and Fall of The Inca Empire" located on the home page.
2. You will be split into groups of three and required to write down five facts on the Inca Empire on the board in front of the class.
3. You will then create a poster with your group based on the information learned about the Inca Empire, implementing color schemes and techniques to accurately represent the Inca Empire
4. Your group will use the remainder of class and the following day in class to present your poster to your classmates and teacher
Objective:

After completion of the assignment students will be able to describe and have a full understanding of the Inca Civilization
