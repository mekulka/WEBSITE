Process

1: First you will answer 5 questions on the video that is located on the home page. The questions are listed as follows.

- Where were the Inca Located?
- What/Who is the sun god?
- Who was the first King of the Incas?
- How many died in the disease outbreak?
- What year was the inca said to be the greatest empire on earth?

2:You will be split into random groups of three.

3: From here you and your group will analyze the information given on the webpage.

4: After your group goes over the information you will be required to write down a total of five facts on the board from what you learned during your analysis.

5: Your group will then get together the required materials needed for your assignment.

6: Lastly, your group will gather the required materials for creating your poster.

7: Each student will provide an equal amount of effort requred for the assignment.

8: Each students grade will be determined by these factors.
Required materials:

1. Webquest page

2. Poster Board

3. Paper

4. Writing Utencils

5. Art Supplies
